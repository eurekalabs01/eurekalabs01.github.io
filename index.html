Mission
Built for curiosity
and rigor

Eureka Labs is a collection of inquiry-based hands-on lab activities that guide learners through authentic scenarios, each highlighting key cybersecurity concepts and principles. Our goal is to provide free, easy-to-use, and high-quality security education materials.

For educators, each lab includes teaching procedures, assessment instruments, and adaptable resources. Our team continuously refines materials to reflect the latest threats, techniques, and best practices in the field.

We call these laboratory projects Eureka Experiences in reference to the "aha!" moment of understanding a previously incomprehensible concept.
